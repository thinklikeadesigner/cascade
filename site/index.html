What Makes This Different

Your to-do list doesn't know about your yearly goal. Your yearly goal doesn't know what you did today. Cascade is the thing in between.

Execution

You don't need another plan. You need today's three tasks and a reason each one matters.

Honesty

Cascade tells you when you're behind. It won't sugarcoat a 40% week.

Adaptation

Had a bad week? Cool, next week's plan accounts for it. The system bends so you don't break.
